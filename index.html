About our Mission

How Quality Education is a human necessity

As the 4th objective of the Sustainable Development Goals, 'Quality Education' is an important aspect of life, which every human being, no matter what their origin, ethnicity, background and wealth is, should be able to experience. Whether you are poor, or rich, quality education is something that every single human should have, as without it, income inequality and poverty could arise. Hence, we, the rockodiles, have taken the initiative to raise awareness about this situation.
